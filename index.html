About the App

KJV Bible & AI Insights gives you the complete King James Version of the Bible in a clean, easy-to-read format, along with AI-powered explanations, a daily verse, a personal journal, bookmarks, and favorites. The full Bible works offline.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an internet connection?
Reading the Bible works fully offline. An internet connection is only needed for AI explanations, which use an online service.

How are AI explanations generated?
When you request an explanation, the selected Bible passage is sent to Google's Gemini AI service to generate a response. The explanations are for informational and study purposes only and are intended to be respectful of all Christian traditions.

Where is my personal data stored?
Your favorites, bookmarks, journal entries, and saved AI notes are stored only on your device. They are not uploaded to any server.

How do I turn off the daily reminder?
Open the app menu, go to Daily Verse, and switch off the daily reminder.

Privacy Policy

This app respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is involved when you use KJV Bible & AI Insights.

Data stored on your device
Your favorites, bookmarks, journal entries, and saved AI notes are stored locally on your device only. They are not uploaded or shared.

AI features
When you request an AI explanation, the selected Bible passage is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate the response. No personal information is attached to these requests.

Advertising
This app displays ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve and measure ads. In regions where required (such as the EU), you will be asked for consent before personalized ads are shown. You can change your choice at any time in the app's Privacy Settings.

Third-party services
This app uses Google AdMob (advertising) and Google Gemini (AI). Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details on how they handle data.

Children
This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
